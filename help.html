Help

Tips and Tricks

1. How can I organize my notes effectively?

Use folders and tags to categorize your notes. This helps in easy retrieval and organization.

2. Are there keyboard shortcuts for quick note-taking?

Yes, you can use shortcuts like Ctrl+B for bold, Ctrl+I for italic, and Ctrl+S to save your notes quickly.

3. Can I set reminders for important notes?

Yes, you can set reminders for specific notes to stay on top of important tasks and deadlines.

Notes

1. How do I create a new note?

Click on the "New Note" button or use the shortcut Ctrl+N to create a new note. You can then start typing your content.

2. Can I format the text in my notes?

Yes, you can format text using options like bold, italic, and underline. Highlight the text and use the formatting toolbar.

3. Is there an option to collaborate on notes with others?

Yes, you can share notes with collaborators, allowing multiple users to work on the same note simultaneously.

Troubleshooting

1. My notes are not syncing across devices. What should I do?

Ensure that you are connected to the internet and that sync is enabled in your account settings. Refresh the app or restart the device if needed.

2. I accidentally deleted a note. Can I recover it?

Check the "Trash" or "Recycle Bin" in your notes app. Deleted notes are often moved to the trash, allowing for recovery within a certain time frame.

3. The app is running slow. How can I improve performance?

Clear unnecessary cache and close unused tabs or applications. If the issue persists, check for updates or contact our support team for assistance.
