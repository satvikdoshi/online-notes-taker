Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our online notes taker service.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide when using our service, including but not limited to your notes, account details, and any feedback you provide.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Your notes and account details are stored securely, and we do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

Security

We take the security of your information seriously and implement measures to protect it. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance your user experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of our service.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@yournotestaker.com.
